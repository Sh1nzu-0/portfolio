About KFC

KFC was founded by Colonel Harland Sanders in 1930 in Kentucky, USA. He became famous for his fried chicken recipe made with a secret blend of 11 herbs and spices.

Over the years, KFC expanded across the world and became one of the largest fast food chains globally, serving millions of customers every day.


Our Mission

KFC aims to serve high-quality fried chicken and delicious meals while creating a fun dining experience for families and customers around the world.
